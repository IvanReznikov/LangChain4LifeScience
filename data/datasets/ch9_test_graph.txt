Drug: Aspirin
Description: Aspirin is a medication used to reduce pain, fever, or inflammation. Specific uses include the treatment of headaches, migraines, and arthritis.

Drug: Ibuprofen
Description: Ibuprofen is a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used for treating pain, fever, and inflammation. It's commonly used for headaches, dental pain, and muscle aches.

Drug: Paracetamol
Description: Paracetamol, also known as acetaminophen, is a medication used to treat pain and fever. It is typically used for mild to moderate pain relief.

Drug: Warfarin
Description: Warfarin is an anticoagulant medication used to treat and prevent blood clots, which can help prevent heart attacks, strokes, and other blood clot-related conditions.
